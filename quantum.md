# Quantum Physics

My early research focused on experimental quantum optics and ultracold atomic physics at the Centre for Quantum Technologies, National University of Singapore.

Key work included:

- frequency-comb-based laser stabilization
- optical metrology systems
- Raman spectroscopy laser platforms
- nonlinear optics with Rydberg atoms
- microwave-to-optical photon conversion

These experiments required building complex physical systems involving optics, RF electronics, vacuum systems, and precision instrumentation.
